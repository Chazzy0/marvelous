    Frequently Asked Questions

    see answer to common question asked and find out more about using the platform

    • 1.Wallet Funding?

      * How long does it take for my Cryptocurrency transfer or deposit to reflect in my wallet? Your Cryptocurrency will be deposited in your wallet after it gets 1 confirmation on the blockchain.
      * Why is the amount showing in my wallet less than what I deposited? A transfer fee could have been deducted from the total Cryptocurrency sent from platform you transferred your cryptocurrency.
      * Why is the balance on Blockchain explorer different from the balance in my Zickie.ng wallet? Zickie.ng employs an off-chain method when handling some Cryptocurrency transactions within the system. Off-chain transaction takes the value outside of the Blockchain and they can be executed instantly.
      Off-chain transactions offer more security and anonymity to the participants because details are not publicly broadcast and most importantly off-chain transactions usually don't have a transaction fee. The transactions may be later reported or batched together before submitted to the main chain.

    • 2.Buying and Selling?

      * Does the platform allow selling Cryptocurrencies in USD value?
      Yes, the USD value of all transaction are on the Sell and Buy page
      * How can I confirm the rate my transaction was processed?
      Rates at which a transaction was processed can be found in your Transaction History
      * Where can I set my Bank Account details?
      You can set your preferred bank account details in the Bank section from the Menu and you can also send Nara to a different account directly from the withdrawal page without changing your preferred bank account.

      * Which documents can I provide to complete my KYC?
      Any upload of your government issued ID (front side and back side) and a selfie of the owner holding the ID card matching your profile name is needed.
      * Why was my upload rejected and how can I ensure the document is approved next time?
      Your KYC upload could have been rejected if the images uploaded are not visible enough for validation, if the name does not match the profile account name, if the picture in the ID and the person holding the ID card do not match, if the ID card is partially blocking part of the users face during selfie or if the ID number typed in and the ID in the upload do not match.
